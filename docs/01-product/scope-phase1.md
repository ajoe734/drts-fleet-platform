# Scope and Phase Boundaries

## Phase 1

- Fleet management foundation
- Dispatch and compliance core
- Tenant and platform operational surfaces
- Fixed-route and reservation-route support at repo-structure level
- Monitoring center, booking platform, and vehicle information platform accommodation at architecture level

## Phase 2

- Autonomous-driving management additions
- FSD and ODD enablement
- Tesla-specific runtime integrations
- Takeover quick report and supervised-mode features for driver operations

## Notes

- This bootstrap does not define production business rules.
- Database schema, pricing logic, payments logic, and other deep workflows remain intentionally undecided.
